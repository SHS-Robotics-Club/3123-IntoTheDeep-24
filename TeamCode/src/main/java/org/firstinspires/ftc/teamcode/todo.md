## Overview
This file maintains a list of high and low priority features that are needed or desired to
improve the robot's function.

### High Priority
The following are high priority improvement to the robot.
* Claw Control - controls the claw servos by defining two positions that the claw may be in: open or closed.
* Claw Arm Control - controls the motor that lifts the claw up and down.
* Claw Slide Control - lifts samples up to one of two baskets using two preset heights.
